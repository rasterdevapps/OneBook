# Tally Features Overview

A Tally enterprise application (currently evolving as TallyPrime 7.0) is far more than just a digital ledger; it is a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution designed to centralize every aspect of business management.

Here is a detailed breakdown of its core features, functionalities, and provisions:

## 1. Core Accounting & Financial Management

At its heart, Tally automates the "Order-to-Cash" and "Procure-to-Pay" cycles.

- **Voucher Entry**: Supports various transaction types (Sales, Purchase, Payment, Receipt, Contra, Journal).
- **Real-time Statements**: Instantly generates Balance Sheets, Profit & Loss accounts, and Cash Flow statements that update with every single entry.
- **Credit Management**: Allows you to set credit limits for customers and monitor "Accounts Receivable" to prevent bad debts.
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Handles transactions in multiple currencies, automatically calculating exchange rate gains or losses.

## 2. Advanced Inventory & Manufacturing

Tally provides granular visibility into the supply chain.

- **Stock Management**: Track stock levels, groups, and categories. It supports multiple Units of Measure (e.g., buying in Kg, selling in Grams).
- **Multi-Location (Godown) Tracking**: Manage inventory across different warehouses or retail outlets.
- **Manufacturing (BOM)**: Define a Bill of Materials (BOM) to track the consumption of raw materials and the production of finished goods.
- **Batch & Expiry**: Critical for the pharmaceutical and food industries, this allows tracking of items by batch numbers and expiration dates.

## 3. Statutory Compliance (GST & Taxation)

Tally is widely known for its "Connected Compliance" ecosystem.

- **GST Handling**: Automated calculation of CGST, SGST, and IGST. It generates GST-compliant invoices and supports E-way bills and E-invoicing.
- **Direct Filing**: Features like Live Portal Sync allow you to reconcile GSTR-2A/2B and upload returns directly to the GST portal.
- **TDS/TCS**: Automated deduction and collection of tax at source, ensuring you never miss a statutory deadline.

## 4. Banking & Cash Management

The "PrimeBanking" feature bridges the gap between your books and your bank.

- **Auto Bank Reconciliation**: Import bank statements (Excel/CSV) to automatically match transactions.
- **Connected Payments**: Direct integration with banks (like SBI, HDFC, Axis) to initiate payments from within the software.
- **Cheque Management**: Integrated cheque printing and a "Cheque Register" to track issued and cancelled cheques.

## 5. Enterprise Security & Audit (Edit Log)

To meet modern regulatory standards (like MCA norms), Tally includes high-level security provisions:

- **Audit Trail (Edit Log)**: A non-tamperable log that records every change made to a transaction—who changed it, when, and what the original value was.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Define specific rights for users (e.g., a Data Entry operator cannot see the Balance Sheet).
- **TallyVault**: An enhanced encryption layer that makes your data invisible to anyone without the specific vault password, even at the file-folder level.

## 6. Connectivity & Cloud Provisions

Tally has shifted from a "desktop-only" model to a "hybrid-cloud" model.

- **TallyDrive**: A secure cloud backup provision that automatically saves your data to the cloud at scheduled intervals.
- **Remote Access**: Access your data from a web browser or a mobile device while your main computer is running in the office.
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Directly share invoices, ledger statements, and reports with customers via WhatsApp.
